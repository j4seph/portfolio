DESIGN PROCESS

INITIAL IDEA

When approaching a new idea or project, first I contemplate it's purpose and the problem it solves. I will then map out on paper it's functions and basic design concepts.

VISUALISATION

Before I grace my keyboard with code, I find it very helpful to create a mock up of my project in Figma or Adobe Illustrator. This allows me to consider all the components it may need and how a user will interact with what's on screen.

IMPLEMENTATION

Finally, once the project has been ironed out on paper and in design, I can then make it come to life using my development skills. This part is made a tonne easier due to the first two steps of my approach, as I already know exactly what I need to build out.
